My background

✻

I am a self-taught web developer living in Bayelsa, Nigeria. Shortly after I acquired my degree in computer science, I developed interests in content creation and photography. As time went by, I got curious about web development and sought to give it a go. Web development became an interesting journey for me and it was only a matter of time before the combination of my other interests began to influence my web design style, as my love for photography and content creation began to play a major role in the way I use images and font styles to communicate the desired messages for my clients.
